# 🏰 THE CONQUEROR
## A Story-Driven Combat Game

---

## 📖 Overview

**The Conqueror** is an interactive narrative-driven combat game featuring rhythm-based battle mechanics, moral choice systems, and dynamic encounters with enemies. Players guide a legendary warrior through a tale of power, honor, and survival while navigating treacherous journeys filled with unexpected Marie patrol encounters.

---
## How to run

Simply download the HTML file in the repository.

---

## 🎮 How to Play

### **Title Screen**
- Select your difficulty level:
  - **NORMAL**: Balanced gameplay
  - **HARD**: Enemy hits harder, more aggressive AI

### **Story Navigation**
- Read dialogue and make moral choices that affect your **HONOUR** rating
- Honour ranges from 0-100 and impacts the story endings
- Higher honour = merciful/heroic path
- Lower honour = ruthless/powerful path

### **Combat System**

#### **Attacking (Your Turn)**
- **Q** - Start your combo attack
- **W/E/R/T/Y/U** - Follow the highlighted keys to continue your combo
- Each correct key increases combo count and speeds up attacks
- **Wrong key or timeout** = Combo drops, enemy attacks

#### **Defense (Enemy Turn)**
- **SPACE** - Press during parry minigame to block attacks
  - Green zone: 37.5%-62.5% of the bar
  - Successful parry = Skip that hit
  - Failed parry = Take damage as normal

#### **Special Ability**
- **E** - Evasion (during enemy attacks only)
  - Skips the ENTIRE enemy turn
  - 2-turn cooldown before reuse
  - Use strategically!

#### **Defense Option**
- **TAB (HOLD)** - Defensive stance (reduces damage taken)

## 🗺️ Story Structure

### **Act 1: Escape**
- **THE PALACE** - Conqueror learns of the King's betrayal
- **THE PRINCE** - Make your first moral choice
  - *Protect His Future* (+20 honour)
  - *Use Him For Power* (-20 honour)
- **TRAVEL TO HIGHLANDS** - First chance for Marie patrol encounter (60%)

### **Act 2: The Highlands**
- **MISSION: BANDIT CAMP** - The job board presents a contract
  - **Lyra** offers her help
  - **MORAL CHOICE**: How to handle the bandits
    - *Detain Them* (+15 honour) - Show mercy
    - *Burn It All Down* (-15 honour) - Ruthless elimination
  - **FIGHT**: Battle the Bandit Leader
  - **TRAVEL BACK** - Another chance for encounter (60%)

### **Act 3: The Revelation**
- **BAR SCENE** - Lyra reveals she's a spy for Marie
- **LYRA COMBAT** - Forced to fight your ally
  - Must defeat her to proceed
- **FINAL CHOICE**: What to do with Lyra after victory
  - *Kill Her* (-30 honour) - Eliminate the threat
  - *Spare Her* (+25 honour) - Show mercy despite betrayal

### **Endings**
- **DARK ENDING** (Honour ≤ 30): Power and dominion through ruthlessness
- **NEUTRAL ENDING** (Honour 31-69): Balance between strength and mercy
- **LIGHT ENDING** (Honour ≥ 70): Heroic redemption and honor

## 🏃 Travel Encounters

During story transitions, the Conqueror travels across dangerous lands:

### **When You Travel**
1. You see the **TRAVEL SCREEN** with a sword animation
2. **60% chance** a Marie patrol blocks your path
3. If encounter: Screen flashes red, combat initiates
4. **Marie Patrol HP**: 200-300 (randomized)
5. Defeat them to continue your journey

### **Travel Points in Game**
- Escaping to the Highlands (after Prince choice)
- Heading to the Bandit Camp (after accepting mission)
- Returning from the Bandit Camp (after defeating leader)

## 🎯 Honour System

Your decisions throughout the game affect your honour rating (0-100):

### **Honour Changes**
| Choice | Change |
|--------|--------|
| Protect the Prince's future | +20 |
| Use the Prince for power | -20 |
| Detain bandits mercifully | +15 |
| Burn bandits ruthlessly | -15 |
| Spare Lyra after defeat | +25 |
| Kill Lyra after defeat | -30 |

### **Honour Display**
- Color bar in top-right corner
- Red (low) → Yellow (medium) → Green (high)
- Affects which ending you receive

## 💡 Tips & Strategies

1. **Combo Timing** - Each successful key increases attack speed. Stay focused!
2. **Parry Practice** - Watch for the green zone. Timing is crucial.
3. **Evasion Cooldown** - Don't waste evasion early. Save it for dangerous moments.
4. **Honour Matters** - Your path affects dialogue and endings. Choose wisely.
